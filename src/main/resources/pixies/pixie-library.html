Type a command in chat and your pixie will walk across the stream overlay. Click a colour dot to preview recolours, then click the command to copy it.

How it works
Type !pixie <name> in chat during the stream. Your pixie spawns on the overlay and walks around — chat messages appear as bubbles above it. You can change your sprite any time by typing the command again.
